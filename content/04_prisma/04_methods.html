5. Eligibility Criteria#

COMPLETED

The inclusion and exclusion criteria are specified within the methods chapter in Inclusion and Data Extraction sections.

6. Information sources#

COMPLETED

The databases used in the study were ACM Digital Library, Scopus, Web of Science, and Pubmed Central. The dates of the last search for each database was the 09/03/2023. Details are listed in the Search Strategy section.

7. Search strategy#

COMPLETED

We have provided full search strings for each database

8. Selection process#

COMPLETED

We have provided full details on the process our two reviewers used to select each study in the section on Study selection

9. Data collection process#

COMPLETED

We have provided full details of the data extraction and audit process that we employed in the research. See Data Extraction

10. Data items#

COMPLETED

We split our review into two parts: data extraction from all papers included, and a more detailed best practice audit of those studies that did share their models and code that would allow us to critically appraise practice.

10a. Primary outcomes#

COMPLETED

We list all information extracted in first part of our review in the Data Extraction section. For our subset analysis, we provide a detailed discussion and presentation of our criteria in Best Practice Audit section.

10b. Other variables#

COMPLETED

We collected additional variables in the first part of our review and these are also listed within Data Extraction. Our focus here was on the platform for sharing and if the studies used a reporting guideline.

11. Study risk of bias assessment#

NOT APPLICABLE

Bias of this type is not applicable to the type of study conducted. A limitation of the study is that we cannot comment on the practice of models shared that are not referenced in the study article.

12. Effect measures#

NOT APPLICABLE

This study does not estimate an effect.
